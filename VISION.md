# Vision — IDA Dataset Factory

## One sentence

Build the factory that continuously produces high-quality datasets for training future IDA language models.

## What we are

An **Automatic Knowledge Factory**:

- Discover trusted sources  
- Collect documents  
- Extract structured knowledge  
- Validate quality  
- Append datasets  
- Export training packages  

## What we are not

- Decision engine  
- Reasoning engine  
- Chatbot  
- Knowledge graph product  
- Executive AI / ECC  
- Agent framework  

Those live in other repositories and **consume** this factory’s exports.

## Success

Measured by:

- Verified rows added  
- Coverage and schema completeness  
- Confidence and freshness  
- Export readiness for fine-tuning  

Not by engineering complexity.
